UF Hardener

UF Hardener is a high-performance Urea Formaldehyde hardening agent designed to improve bonding strength, curing performance, durability, and stability in wood adhesives, particle boards, laminates, and moulding compounds.

Download Catalogue

Product Overview

Latent hardener for urea formaldehyde impregnation resins with long pot-life and high curing activity. UF Hardener (Urea Formaldehyde Hardener) is widely used in the wood and furniture industry as a catalyst and curing agent that enhances the strength and durability of UF resins. It ensures fast curing, excellent bonding, smooth finishing, and superior water resistance when used in particle board, plywood, laminates, MDF, and moulding applications.

Instructions for Use

  • The hardener should be thoroughly mixed with the finished resin after it has been cooled down to the required processing temperature.
  • It is recommended to add the curing agent to the resin shortly before a new bath is required for impregnation.
