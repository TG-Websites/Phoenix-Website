Anti-Blocking Additive

An advanced surface-enhancing additive engineered to reduce film-to-film adhesion, improve roll release, and maintain smooth processing in high-speed extrusion lines.

Download Catalogue

Product Overview

Anti-Blocking Additive is formulated to prevent adhesion between polymer film layers during winding, packaging, and high-temperature processing. It creates controlled micro-roughness on the film surface, improving slip properties without affecting clarity or strength. Ideal for modern flexible packaging, lamination, and multilayer film applications.
