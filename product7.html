MF Hardener

MF Hardener is a high-strength melamine-formaldehyde based hardening agent designed to improve durability, adhesion, and surface hardness in coatings, laminates, wood polishing systems, and industrial resin applications.

Product Overview

MF Hardener (Melamine Formaldehyde Hardener) is a premium cross-linking agent used to enhance hardness, chemical resistance, gloss, and mechanical strength in industrial coatings, wood finishes, paper resins, laminates, and adhesives. It provides excellent curing properties and significantly improves product performance.

Technical Specifications

Appearance Clear to Light Yellow Liquid
Solid Content 52% – 55%
pH Value 8.0 – 9.5
Viscosity 50 – 150 cps

Applications

  • Wood Coatings & Polishes
  • Industrial Laminates (HPL & LPL)
  • Melamine-based Resins
  • Paper Impregnation
  • Surface Coatings & Decorative Finishes
  • Furniture & Panel Industry
  • Adhesives & Bonding Applications

Key Benefits

High Surface Hardness

Improves scratch resistance and overall hardness.

Excellent Chemical Resistance

Protects surfaces from stains, solvents & moisture.

Enhanced Gloss & Finish

Provides a smooth, shiny, premium finish for surfaces.

Fast Curing Performance

Reduces drying time and increases production efficiency.
