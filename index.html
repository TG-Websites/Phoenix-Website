About Us

Phoenix Additives Chemicals, established in 2017, is one of India’s leading suppliers and importers of specialty additive chemicals, catering exclusively to the Laminate and Pre-Laminate industry. We specialize in providing high-quality additives used in decorative low-pressure pre-laminated MDF, particle boards, and high-pressure laminates — ensuring superior finish, durability, and performance.

With a strong focus on the Indian market, we have become the first choice for major laminate and wood panel manufacturers across the country. Built on fairness, transparency, and customer-centric practices, Phoenix Additives Chemicals ensures consistent quality, reliable imports, and prompt service — delivering not just products, but complete solutions.

Our Mission

To provide the Indian laminate and pre-laminate industry with high-quality specialty additive chemicals through fair trade, reliable supply, and customer-focused solutions. We aim to bridge the gap between global innovation and local manufacturing needs, ensuring that our clients receive consistent quality, convenience, and value in every delivery.

Our Vision

To be recognized as India’s most trusted and preferred partner for specialty additive chemicals — bridging global innovation with Indian manufacturing excellence. We aim to continuously enhance our product portfolio, service standards, and ethics to support the growth, sustainability, and competitiveness of India’s decorative surface industry.
