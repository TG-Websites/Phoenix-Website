UF Hardener

UF Hardener is a high-performance Urea Formaldehyde hardening agent designed to improve bonding strength, curing performance, durability, and stability in wood adhesives, particle boards, laminates, and moulding compounds.

Download Catalogue

Product Overview

UF Hardener (Urea Formaldehyde Hardener) is widely used in the wood and furniture industry as a catalyst and curing agent that enhances the strength and durability of UF resins. It ensures fast curing, excellent bonding, smooth finishing, and superior water resistance when used in particle board, plywood, laminates, MDF, and moulding applications.
