Plate Cleaning Agent

High-performance Plate Cleaning Agent formulated to remove ink, stains, grease, and residues from plates used in printing and industrial applications. Delivers efficient cleaning without damaging metal surfaces.

Product Overview

Plate Cleaning Agent is a specially formulated cleaning solution designed to remove stubborn ink residues, grease, oil deposits, carbon layers, and contaminants from metal plates and printing plates. It restores surface brightness, enhances print quality, and improves plate life by ensuring smooth, residue-free surfaces.

Technical Specifications

Appearance Clear or Slightly Yellow Liquid
pH Value 7.0 – 9.0
Solubility Completely Soluble in Water
Flash Point Non-Flammable

Applications

  • Offset Printing Plates Cleaning
  • Metal Plate Cleaning in Industrial Units
  • Removal of Ink, Grease & Carbon Residues
  • Used in Printing, Packaging & Coating Industries
  • Cleaning of Roller, Drum & Machine Surfaces

Key Benefits

Deep Cleaning

Quickly removes stubborn ink and oil residues.

Non-Corrosive

Safe on metal surfaces and extends plate life.

Fast Drying

Leaves no residue and ensures faster turnaround time.

Eco-Friendly

Low-odor and environmentally safe formulation.
