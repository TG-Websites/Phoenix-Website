MF Hardener

MF Hardener is a high-strength melamine-formaldehyde based hardening agent designed to improve durability, adhesion, and surface hardness in coatings, laminates, wood polishing systems, and industrial resin applications.

Product Overview

MF Hardener (Melamine Formaldehyde Hardener) is a premium cross-linking agent used to enhance hardness, chemical resistance, gloss, and mechanical strength in industrial coatings, wood finishes, paper resins, laminates, and adhesives. It provides excellent curing properties and significantly improves product performance.

Instructions

  • The hardener should be thoroughly mixed with the finished resin after it has been cooled to the required processing temperature.
  • It is recommended to add the curing agent to the resin shortly before a new bath is required for impregnation.
  • The resin mixture should be adjusted to achieve a turbidity time of 3–5 minutes at 100°C.
  • For optimal performance, add approximately 0.20 – 0.50% curing agent relative to the liquid resin quantity.

Key Benefits

Decreased Pressing Time

Significantly reduces pressing time, enabling faster production cycles and increased output efficiency.

Accelerated Drying

Speeds up the condensation process in the drying oven, resulting in shorter drying times.

Prevents Resin Sagging

Prevents sagging of the resin during processing, ensuring a smooth, uniform, and even coating.

Maintains Pot Life

Extends the pot life of resins containing curing agents, allowing better planning, handling, and material utilization.
