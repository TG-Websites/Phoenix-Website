About Us

Phoenix Additives Chemicals is driven by a singular vision — to expand globally while strengthening our footprint across India. Since inception, we have been actively involved in the import and merchandise trade of highly specialized additive chemicals used in the production of Decorative High-Pressure Laminates, Pre-Laminated MDF, Particle Boards, and Plywood Industries. Guided by our commitment to Fair Trade Practices, we have consistently elevated our growth and expanded our business network nationwide.

We import premium-grade chemicals from leading international markets including Spain, Germany, France, Turkey, and more. These materials are supplied to top manufacturers at both domestic and overseas levels, ensuring reliable quality and consistent performance. Alongside imports, Phoenix Additives Chemicals is actively engaged in Interim Trading and Domestic Trading, sourcing and supplying merchandise that meets the evolving needs of India’s laminate and wood panel industries.

As a pioneer in Third-Country Trade, we specialize in global buying and selling operations that optimize supply chains and ensure uninterrupted access to high-quality materials. Moving ahead, our goal is to expand the horizon of our business spectrum with determination, professionalism, and a global mindset. We remain committed to serving high-quality-focused industries with excellence, transparency, and dependable service.

Our Mission

To provide the Indian laminate and pre-laminate industry with high-quality specialty additive chemicals through fair trade, reliable supply, and customer-focused solutions. We aim to bridge the gap between global innovation and local manufacturing needs, ensuring that our clients receive consistent quality, convenience, and value in every delivery.

Our Vision

To be recognized as India’s most trusted and preferred partner for specialty additive chemicals — bridging global innovation with Indian manufacturing excellence. We aim to continuously enhance our product portfolio, service standards, and ethics to support the growth, sustainability, and competitiveness of India’s decorative surface industry.
