High-Gloss Surface Enhancers

It is an additive for increasing the gloss value obtained. It can be added directly to melamine, urea, water-based resin mixtures.

Product Overview

High-Gloss Surface Enhancers improve final product appearance by increasing shine, depth, and reflectiveness. Designed for plastic, paint, coating, and polish manufacturers, this additive enhances the optical characteristics while maintaining high stability and durability.
